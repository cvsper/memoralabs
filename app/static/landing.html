3

Search & Recall

Query with natural language. Results are ranked by semantic relevance with temporal decay.

Features

  • Semantic Search Vector similarity, not keyword matching — your agents find what they mean, not just what they typed.
  • Entity Extraction Automatic entity and relation detection so your memory graph builds itself.
  • Multi-tenant Isolation Each developer gets their own encrypted data store. Your data never touches another tenant's.
  • Usage Tracking Monitor your quota with every search response. Know exactly where you stand.
  • Self-Improving Retrieval quality improves as you use it. Coming soon.
